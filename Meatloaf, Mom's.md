# Mom's Meatloaf

- 3 lb ground beef
- 1 can tomato soup concentrate
- 3 eggs
- 1 medium onion, diced
- 4 stalks celery, chopped
- 1 tbs beef boullion
- 1 tsp marjoram
- 1 tsp thyme
- 2 tbsp parsley
- 1/2 tsp sage
- 2-3 sleeves Ritz crackers, crushed

## Instructions

Combine ingredients, form loaf, and coat with ketchup.

Bake at 350 for 90-120 minutes.
